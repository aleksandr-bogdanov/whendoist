## About Whendoist

**Whendoist** is a task scheduling app that answers the question: *"WHEN do I do my tasks?"*

While other tools tell you *what* to do (task lists) or *when* you're busy (calendars), Whendoist bridges the gap — helping you decide *when* to actually do your tasks.

### Key Features

- **Smart Scheduling** — Drag tasks directly onto your calendar
- **Energy Modes** — Filter tasks by mental energy required
- **Google Calendar Integration** — See all your events alongside tasks
- **Plan My Day** — Auto-schedule tasks with one click
- **Privacy-First** — Optional end-to-end encryption

### Target Audience

Professionals, freelancers, and knowledge workers who struggle with time management and want a visual, intuitive way to plan their days.

## Brand Assets

### Logo Usage

The Whendoist logo consists of the **W icon** + **"hendoist"** wordmark.

| Variant | Use Case | File |
|---------|----------|------|
| **Full Color** | Primary use on light backgrounds | `wordmark-light-*.png` |
| **Inverse** | Dark backgrounds | `wordmark-dark-*.png` |
| **Icon Only** | Favicons, avatars, small spaces | `w-icon-*.png` |

### Minimum Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the W icon.

```
┌──────────────────────────────────┐
│                                  │
│    [W] hendoist                  │  ← Clear space = W height
│                                  │
└──────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Minimum Size

- **Full Wordmark:** 100px width minimum
- **Icon Only:** 16px minimum (favicon), 32px recommended

## Typography

### Brand Font

**Quicksand** — A geometric sans-serif with rounded terminals.

- **Source:** Google Fonts
- **Weights:** 400 (body), 500 (wordmark), 600 (emphasis), 700 (headlines)

### Usage

```css
font-family: 'Quicksand', system-ui, sans-serif;
```

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do

- Use official brand colors
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Use high-resolution assets for print
- Link to whendoist.com when featuring the brand

### Don't

- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the logo colors
- Add effects (shadows, gradients) to the logo
- Use the logo on busy backgrounds without contrast
- Recreate or approximate the logo
