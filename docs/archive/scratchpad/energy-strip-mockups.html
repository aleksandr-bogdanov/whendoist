Comparison Matrix

Option Content cost Visual weight Discoverability Apple-native feel Dark mode
1. Solid strip ~36px fixed Heavy High Low (web-app feel) Fine
2. Glass compact 0px (floats) Minimal Medium High Great
3. Glass wide 0px (floats) Medium High High Great
4. Segmented 0px (floats) Medium High Highest Good
5. In-bar 0px Low Low Low Fine
Recommendation: Option 2 (Glass compact) or Option 4 (Segmented).

Glass compact — if you want maximum content, minimum chrome, and the Apple glass vibe. The emojis are already learned; no labels needed after day one.

Segmented — if you want it to feel like a native iOS control that anyone immediately understands. Slightly more visual weight but extremely clear affordance.

Both float, both are zero-cost on content area, both look great in dark mode.
